Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive architectural and design services including residential design, commercial architecture, interior design, Vastu consultation, 3D modeling, 2D layouts, site execution, and civil work.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on complexity and scope. Small interior projects may take 2-4 months, while large residential projects can take 8-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Yes, we specialize in 3D modeling and visualization services. We create realistic renderings, walkthrough videos, and virtual tours to help you visualize your project before construction.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, and services required. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements during the initial consultation.

Do you work outside Indore?

Yes, we work on projects across Madhya Pradesh and neighboring states. We can provide remote consultation and on-site visits as needed for out-of-city projects.

Do you handle construction supervision?

Yes, we provide complete project management including site execution, quality control, and construction supervision to ensure your project is completed to the highest standards.
